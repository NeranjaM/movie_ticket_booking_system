Jawan-Hindi Movie

Jaawan is a 2023 Indian Hindi language action thriller film. It features Deepika Padukone (as a special model), Priyamani and Sanya Malhotra in supporting roles.A high-octane action thriller that outlines the emotional journey of a man who is set to rectify the wrongs in society.
